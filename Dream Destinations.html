Why Switzerland?

Switzerland has always been my dream destination. From the breathtaking Swiss Alps covered in snow to the peaceful lakes and green valleys, Switzerland feels like a fairy-tale land. I want to experience the beauty of cities like Zurich and Geneva, ride the scenic trains, and taste the world-famous Swiss chocolates.
