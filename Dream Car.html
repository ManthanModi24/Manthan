About the Jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko is a Swedish hypercar built for speed, innovation, and performance. Designed as a successor to the Agera RS, it pushes the limits of automotive engineering with advanced aerodynamics, cutting-edge materials, and an engine that delivers mind-blowing power.

Why Koenigsegg?

Koenigsegg is known for redefining the boundaries of what cars can achieve. With constant innovations like Freevalve technology, lightweight carbon fiber design, and extreme performance, Koenigsegg is not just building cars – it’s shaping the future of speed and engineering.
